About Me

Hi, I’m Anshu Singh, a B.Tech CSE student at IILM University, Greater Noida, with a strong passion for web development and UI/UX design.

I’m currently focused on mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also exploring how thoughtful UI/UX design can turn simple websites into meaningful user experiences. I enjoy building clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces that not only look good but feel intuitive to use.

As a learner, I believe in growing step by step — from understanding core web fundamentals to moving towards advanced frontend technologies and modern design systems. I’m constantly experimenting, learning, and improving through hands-on projects and real-world inspired designs.

In the future, my goal is to become a skilled frontend developer and UI/UX designer, capable of designing and developing complete digital products from scratch.

When I’m not coding or designing, I enjoy sports and creative work, which helps me stay disciplined, focused, and inspired.
